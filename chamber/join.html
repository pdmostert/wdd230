Membership Benefits

Thank you for your interest in joining the Border-Kei Chamber of Commerce! Please select your preferred membership type from the options below and fill out the required information.

1. Nonprofit (NP) Membership:

This membership type is for nonprofit organizations. There is no membership fee associated with this category. Nonprofit members enjoy essential benefits that foster community growth and collaboration.

2. Bronze Membership:

Bronze members receive basic benefits to support their business needs. This membership level offers essential resources for networking and community engagement.

3. Silver Membership:

Silver members enjoy enhanced benefits, including access to exclusive events and promotional opportunities. This membership level is designed for businesses seeking additional exposure and networking opportunities.

4. Gold Membership:

Gold members receive premium benefits, including priority access to all Chamber events, personalized marketing support, and increased visibility within the community. Gold membership is ideal for businesses aiming for maximum exposure and growth.
